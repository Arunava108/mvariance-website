Advanced Electronics Manufacturing & Assembly Workshop

Course Overview

This hands-on course delves into Advanced Electronics Manufacturing & Assembly, offering comprehensive training on the entire inline assembly process. Participants will gain practical experience in operating essential equipment, including:

  • Solder Paste Printer
  • Solder Paste Inspection System (SPI)
  • Pick & Place Machine
  • Reflow Oven
  • Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) System

Hands-On Training

  • Tailor-Made PCB Assembly: Assemble SMT components on a custom-designed PCB, ensuring real-world application of skills.
  • Component Handling: Learn ESD-safe methods for handling components in the assembly line.
  • Soldering Techniques: Master various soldering methods essential for high-quality PCBA.
  • Solder Paste Fundamentals: Understand the fundamentals of solder paste and its role in assembly.
  • Stencil Fundamentals: Gain knowledge of stencil design, usage, and maintenance for precise solder paste deposition.

Course Objectives

  • Comprehensive Understanding of the automatic assembly process using both SMT and through-hole components.
  • Operational Proficiency in handling and maintaining key assembly machinery.
  • Practical Skills in assembling PCBs, ensuring participants can apply their knowledge effectively.
  • Safety Awareness in component handling and assembly processes to prevent damage and ensure a safe working environment.
